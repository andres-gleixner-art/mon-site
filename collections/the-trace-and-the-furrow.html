      The Trace and the Furrow

      La trace et le sillon · 2004–2007

      Shaped energy. Plaster meets the work as a versatile surface; the trace, like a writing, makes painting the literature of its own history — my furrow.

      This series evokes the search for identity in its roots.

      Materials: plaster, oil, acrylic.
